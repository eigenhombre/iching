Clojure 1.9 or above.

$ clojure -Aoracle

Number 3 屯 (zhūn)
'Sprouting'. Other variations include 'difficulty at the beginning',
'gathering support', and 'hoarding'. Its inner (lower) trigram is ☳
(震 zhèn) shake = (雷) thunder, and its outer (upper) trigram is ☵ (坎
kǎn) gorge = (水) water.
